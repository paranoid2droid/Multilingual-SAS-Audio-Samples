# Audio Samples for Mandarin Speaker Anonymization

This page provides audio samples from our experiments on speaker anonymization for Mandarin speech. The samples are drawn from the AISHELL-3 test set and processed using different anonymization methods. The available methods are presented in the following order:

1. **Original**: Unmodified AISHELL-3 test utterances.
2. **VPC Baseline B2**: Anonymization using the McAdams coefficient.
3. **HU-EN Resynthesis**: English fine-tuned HuBERT with resynthesis.
4. **HU-JA Resynthesis**: Japanese fine-tuned HuBERT with resynthesis.
5. **mHU-JA Resynthesis**: Multilingual HuBERT (mHU-JA) with resynthesis.
6. **HU-EN Selection**: English fine-tuned HuBERT with selection-based anonymizer.
7. **HU-JA Selection**: Japanese fine-tuned HuBERT with selection-based anonymizer.
8. **mHU-JA Selection**: Multilingual HuBERT (mHU-JA) with selection-based anonymizer.
9. **HU-EN OHNN**: English fine-tuned HuBERT with OHNN-based anonymizer.
10. **HU-JA OHNN**: Japanese fine-tuned HuBERT with OHNN-based anonymizer.
11. **mHU-JA OHNN**: Multilingual HuBERT (mHU-JA) with OHNN-based anonymizer.
